Project Information

This project leverages the power of smart contracts deployed on the BSC Testnet to manage exclusive access liquidity pools. Users can connect their MetaMask wallets to register trades, create exclusive pools, and add liquidity seamlessly.

The smart contract interacts with PancakeSwap, allowing for efficient trading and liquidity management, empowering users to participate in decentralized finance effectively.

Incorporating the Brevis ZK-Coprocessor, we ensure enhanced privacy features, enabling users to verify eligibility for exclusive pools without revealing sensitive transaction data.

By utilizing the BSC Testnet (0x9A082015c919AD0E47861e5Db9A1c7070E81A2C7), we provide a risk-free environment for testing and deploying our innovative smart contracts.
